# Quick Start Guide - Fate Create

## 🚀 Getting Started in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Open the App
Simply open `index.html` in your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari).

### Step 2: Create Your First Character
1. Click the **NEW** button
2. Choose **Quick Setup** for guided creation
3. Follow the wizard through each step

### Step 3: Save Your Character
When you reach the character sheet, click **Save Character** to store it in your browser.

---

## 📝 Character Creation Walkthrough

### The Wizard Steps

#### 1️⃣ High Concept (Step 1)
- **What it is**: A phrase that sums up your character
- **Example**: "Wizard Private Eye" or "Knight of the Round Table"
- **Tip**: Make it interesting and descriptive!

#### 2️⃣ Trouble (Step 2)
- **What it is**: Something that complicates your character's life
- **Example**: "Sucker for a Pretty Face" or "The King's Stooges Are on My Trail"
- **Tip**: This should create interesting story complications

#### 3️⃣ Phase Trio (Steps 3-5)
- **What it is**: Three important moments or relationships in your character's past
- **Example**: "I Owe the Mob a Favor" or "Saved by My Best Friend"
- **Tip**: These connect your character to the world and other characters

#### 4️⃣ Skills (Step 6)
- **What it is**: Your character's capabilities
- **The Pyramid**:
  - 1 skill at +4 (Great)
  - 2 skills at +3 (Good)
  - 3 skills at +2 (Fair)
  - 4 skills at +1 (Average)
- **Tip**: Choose skills that match your character concept

#### 5️⃣ Stunts (Step 7)
- **What it is**: Special tricks or abilities
- **You get**: 3 free stunts
- **Extra stunts**: Cost 1 refresh each (minimum refresh is 1)
- **Tip**: Stunts should give you bonuses or let you break the rules in specific situations

## 💾 Saving & Loading

### Saving Characters
- Click **Save Character** on the character sheet
- Characters are saved to your browser's localStorage
- They persist even after closing the browser

### Loading Characters
1. Click **LOAD** from the home page
2. See all your saved characters
3. Click **Load** on the character you want
4. Edit and save again as needed

### Deleting Characters
- On the Load page, click **Delete** next to any character
- Confirm the deletion (this cannot be undone!)

## 📊 Understanding Your Character Sheet

### Aspects (5 total)
Your character's defining features that can help or hinder them.

### Skills
Rated from +0 to +4, representing your character's capabilities.

### Stunts
Special abilities that give bonuses or unique effects.

### Stress Tracks
- **Physical**: Based on Physique skill
- **Mental**: Based on Will skill
- Check boxes when you take stress in conflicts

### Consequences
- **Mild** (2 shifts)
- **Moderate** (4 shifts)
- **Severe** (6 shifts)
- Write in aspects when you take lasting harm

### Refresh
The number of fate points you start each session with (default: 3).

## 🎯 Pro Tips

### For Aspects
✅ Make them double-edged (can help AND hinder)
✅ Be specific and evocative
✅ Think about story potential
❌ Don't make them too generic

### For Skills
✅ Match your character concept
✅ Consider what you'll do in the game
✅ Balance combat and non-combat skills
❌ Don't min-max too hard

### For Stunts
✅ Focus on what makes your character unique
✅ Tie them to your aspects
✅ Make them mechanically useful
❌ Don't make them too narrow

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Character won't save?
- Make sure you've filled in at least the character name
- Check that your browser allows localStorage
- Try a different browser if issues persist

### Lost my characters?
- Characters are stored per-browser
- Clearing browser data will delete them
- Use the same browser to access saved characters

### Button is disabled?
- Some buttons require you to fill in required fields first
- For example, "Next" buttons need valid input before enabling

## 🎲 FATE Core Basics

### What are Aspects?
Phrases that describe your character, usable for bonuses or complications.

### What are Fate Points?
Currency you spend to invoke aspects for bonuses. You start each session with your refresh value.

### What is the Ladder?
The rating scale in FATE:
- +4 Great
- +3 Good
- +2 Fair
- +1 Average
- +0 Mediocre
- -1 Poor
- -2 Terrible

## 🆘 Need Help?

### Common Questions

**Q: Can I edit a character after saving?**
A: Yes! Load the character and make changes, then save again.

**Q: Can I export my character?**
A: Currently characters are saved in your browser. Export features coming in future updates!

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: Yes! Once loaded, the app works completely offline.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes! Everything is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
